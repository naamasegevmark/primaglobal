ABOUT PRIMA

Our modern cladding solution integrates aesthetic excellence with sustainable, high-performance materials, whilst ensuring timely project delivery.

02

PRIMA'S INNOVATION

Creation of large façade modules without any need for concrete casting or a concrete wall backing, reducing precast wall load by up to 75%.

We offer:

  • ➤ Detailed engineering design tailored to architectural **aspiration**.
  • ➤ Two **installation** methods: alongside structure or **after**.
  • ➤ **Eco-friendly** – 30% less concrete, locally manufactured.
