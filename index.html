ABOUT PRIMA GLOBAL

Prima Global emerged from a fundamental need to drive innovation within the building cladding sector. Our mission is to engineer exteriors that embody both resilience and accuracy.

We developed a new construction method that replaces traditional, cumbersome techniques with a streamlined, high-performance approach.

We offer industrial, pre-cast U/HPC cladding system that deliver both design flexibility and structural performance.

Our patented system solution combines advanced engineering and efficiency to meet top standards of safety, sustainability, durability and cost-effectiveness.

At Prima Global we go beyond simply supplying panels, we deliver a new standard.

PRIMA'S INNOVATION

At Prima Global, our pioneering façade modules eliminate heavy concrete backing, reducing wall loads by up to 75%. This breakthrough accelerates construction, improves sustainability and offers architects unparalleled design flexibility.

WHAT DO WE OFFER YOU?

Detailed Engineering Design

Custom-tailored engineering design, crafted precisely to bring every unique architectural vision and aspiration to life, ensuring optimal performance and aesthetics.

Flexible Installation Methods

Choose between two efficient installation approaches – integrating during construction or applying after – for maximum project flexibility and adaptability.

Eco-Friendly Solutions

Committed to sustainability, our modules use 30% less concrete, significantly reducing carbon footprint. All components are locally manufactured, boosting economies and cutting emissions for a greener construction.
